═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                            KEY BUSINESS RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. ⚠️  MANDATORY: At least 1 substitute arrangement required
2. ✅  ALL-OR-NOTHING: ALL substitutes must accept to proceed
3. ❌  ANY REJECTION: Any rejection = entire request rejected
4. 🔒  LOCKED: Cannot edit/withdraw after substitutes approve
5. 📊  TRANSPARENT: Real-time status tracking for all parties
6. ⏱️  SEQUENTIAL: Must complete substitute approval before HOD review
7. 🔄  CASCADING: HOD → Dean → Principal approval chain
